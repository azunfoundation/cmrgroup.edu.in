Beyond the Classroom

Life at CMR Group

We believe in the holistic development of every student — combining academic excellence with leadership, creativity, and community service.

SRADDHA – Grievance Cell
A student's Grievance Redressal Cell ensuring transparency in Teaching-Learning processes. Provides a formal mechanism for students to report unfair practices with complete confidentiality and fairness.
NSS – National Service Scheme
Active NSS units across CMRGI colleges conducting blood donation drives, tree plantation campaigns, health awareness initiatives, and community upliftment activities throughout the year.
Cultural Fests & Events
Annual college fests — Carpediem (CMRCET), Scintillations (CMRIT), Efflorescence (CMRTC), Illuminate (CMRIT) — featuring music, dance, drama, art and literary competitions.
Technical Symposiums
Inter-college and national-level technical events, paper presentations, hackathons, quiz competitions, and project expos conducted annually to foster innovation and technical skills.
Sports & Games
A well-equipped sports complex supporting cricket, football, badminton, table tennis, volleyball, basketball and more. Annual sports week with inter-college competitions and championships.
International Study Tours
Annual international educational tours to countries like Singapore, Malaysia, Dubai, UK, and more — exposing students to global industries, research labs, and cultural experiences.
Industry Internships
Mandatory internship programmes with 200+ industry partners. Students spend 4-8 weeks in real work environments, gaining practical exposure to engineering, management, and pharma sectors.
Student Entrepreneurs
CMR's MSME Incubation Centre supports student startups with mentorship, seed funding, legal support, and workspace. 20+ student-led startups launched since 2015.
Academic Clubs & Chapters
IEEE Student Branches, CSI Chapters, IETE Chapters, ACM Student Chapters and domain clubs such as AI Club, Robotics Club, Pharma Club and more across all CMRGI campuses.
