Who We Are

Committed to Academic Excellence

CMR Group of Institutions is one of India's premier educational groups, dedicated to imparting quality education and promoting excellence in academic pursuits in the fields of Science, Pharmacy, Engineering, Technology, and Management.

Established under the aegis of the MGR Educational Society, the group is headquartered at Kandlakoya, Medchal Road, Hyderabad — one of India's most dynamic educational hubs.

With six specialized colleges under one umbrella, CMR Group nurtures over 20,000 students annually, guided by 1,500+ expert faculty members. We are consistently recognized among the Top 10 Colleges in Telangana.

Our Foundation

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision, curriculum, and initiative at CMR Group of Institutions.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in education, research, and student outcomes — consistently pushing boundaries of academic achievement.

Innovation

Our DSIR-recognized R&D center and ARIIA #5 ranking reflect our commitment to fostering a culture of creativity and discovery.

Integrity

We build trust through transparent governance, ethical practices, and accountability to our students, parents, and society.

Inclusivity

We welcome students from all backgrounds, ensuring equal opportunity for every learner to discover and achieve their potential.

Empowerment

We equip students with not just knowledge, but the skills, confidence, and network needed to lead in the real world.

Community

Active social outreach, NSS activities, and community programs reflect our responsibility as an institution to give back.
