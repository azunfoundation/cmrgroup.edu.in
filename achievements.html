#2 Private Engineering College in Telangana
CMRCET ranked 2nd among Private Engineering Colleges in Telangana by Times of India, recognizing academic quality and placement performance.

CMR Group's Institute Innovation Cell (IIC) received a 4-Star rating from MHRD for 2025-26 — recognising outstanding innovation and entrepreneurship culture.

CMR Group ranked in Band 5 of the Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements (ARIIA) — Excellent band for Private Engineering Institutions.

B. Sai Deepak, CMRGI student, achieved two Guinness World Records in Taekwondo — a rare feat bringing national recognition to the institution.
