Campus Living

Safe, Secure & Comfortable

CMR Group offers state-of-the-art hostel facilities within the campus premise to provide a comfortable living environment that fosters academic growth and personal development.

Boys Hostel

Our boys' hostel is a modern multi-storied building designed to accommodate 1000+ students. It features spacious, well-ventilated rooms with study tables, chairs, and individual wardrobes.

  • Spacious and well-maintained rooms
  • High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Recreation room with indoor games & TV
  • 24/7 RO purified drinking water
  • Gymnasium access
  • Dedicated resident wardens

Girls Hostel

The girls' hostel offers a highly secure and comfortable living space for over 1000 female students. Every aspect of the facility caters to the ease and well-being of its residents.

  • High security with CCTV and female guards
  • In-house cafeteria and mess
  • Study halls and common areas
  • Emergency medical assistance
  • Lively cultural events and celebrations
  • Washing and laundry facilities

A Complete Experience

Hostel Facilities

Dining & Mess
Hygienic and nutritious meals prepared in highly mechanized kitchens. Both North Indian and South Indian cuisines are served.
Medical Center
On-campus hospital with a dedicated medical officer available 24x7. Free ambulance service for emergency care.
Sports & Fitness
Access to the large campus playground, indoor stadium, yoga center, and outdoor gym for physical well-being.
