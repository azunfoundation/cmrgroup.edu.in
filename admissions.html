How to Apply

Admission Procedure

Follow these simple steps to secure your seat at one of the CMR Group's premier institutions for the 2025-26 academic year.

1

Appear in Qualifying Examination

Write EAMCET / ECET / ICET / PGECET for Engineering. For MBBS, appear in NEET. For Pharmacy, write the relevant state entrance exams. Ensure you meet the minimum eligibility criteria for your chosen program.

2

Register for CMRGI Counselling

Submit your enquiry form on this page or contact the admissions office directly. Our counsellors will guide you through the college and program selection based on your rank and preferences.

3

Document Verification

Carry original certificates for verification — 10th, 12th / Intermediate mark sheets, entrance exam hall ticket & rank card, caste certificate (if applicable), transfer certificate, passport photos, and Aadhaar card.

4

Fee Payment & Seat Confirmation

Pay the prescribed tuition and other fees to confirm your seat. Fee payment can be made via DD, online transfer, or at the institution's accounts office. Seat is confirmed only after fee payment.

5

Orientation & Commencement

Attend the induction / orientation program on the specified date. Collect your ID card, library card, and class timetable. Your journey at CMR begins here!

Who Can Apply

Eligibility Criteria

Minimum qualifications required for admission to our various programs.

B.Tech / B.E. Programs

  • 10+2 / Intermediate with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Minimum 45% aggregate (40% for reserved categories)
  • Valid EAMCET / JEE rank required
  • ECET rank accepted for Lateral Entry

B.Pharmacy / Pharm.D

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology / Mathematics
  • Minimum 45% aggregate in PCB/PCM
  • Valid EAMCET (Pharmacy) rank
  • PCI norms to be satisfied

MBBS Program

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Minimum 50% aggregate in PCB (40% SC/ST)
  • Valid NEET-UG score required
  • Age: 17 years by 31st December of admission year

MBA Program

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Minimum 50% at graduation level
  • Valid ICET / CAT / MAT / CMAT score preferred
  • ICET counselling eligible

MCA Program

  • Bachelor's degree with Mathematics at 10+2 or graduation
  • Minimum 50% at graduation level
  • Valid ICET score accepted
  • PGECET score also considered

M.Tech / M.Pharmacy

  • B.Tech / B.E. in relevant discipline (for M.Tech)
  • B.Pharmacy for M.Pharmacy programs
  • Valid GATE / PGECET score
  • Minimum 50% in qualifying degree
