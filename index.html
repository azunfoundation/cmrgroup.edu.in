Who We Are

Welcome to CMR Group of Institutions

CMR Group of Institutions is one of the premier educational institutions dedicated to imparting quality education and promoting excellence in academic pursuits in the fields of Science, Pharmacy, Engineering, Technology and Management.

Established in 2002, our campuses are located in Kandlakoya, Medchal Road, Hyderabad, Telangana. We are recognized among the Top 10 Colleges in Telangana.
