Commute with Ease

Extensive Bus Network

We operate a massive fleet of modern buses across all major routes in Hyderabad to ensure a safe, comfortable, and punctual commute for all our students and staff.

City-Wide Coverage

Our transport network covers almost every corner of Hyderabad and Secunderabad. We systematically map routes to ensure minimal travel time and maximum convenience.

  • Extensive network of 100+ buses
  • Covers ECIL, LB Nagar, Mehdipatnam, Kukatpally
  • Dedicated routes for faculty and staff
  • Regular maintenance and safety checks

Safety & Technology

The safety of our students is paramount. Every bus is equipped with modern tracking and safety features so parents and students can track commutes in real-time.

  • GPS-enabled live tracking system
  • Trained, experienced, and background-verified drivers
  • First-aid kits installed in every bus
  • Speed governors strictly enforced

Public Connectivity

Other Commute Options

TSRTC Buses
Regular TSRTC buses run from Secunderabad and Medchal right up to the campus entrance bus stop.
MMTS Train Network
Medchal railway station connects to the MMTS local network, making it convenient for long-distance city commuters.
Private Vehicles
Ample, secure parking space is available on campus for students and staff using personal cars and two-wheelers.
