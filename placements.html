Placement Office

Think Placements, Think CMR

A full-fledged Training & Placement wing is established to cater to placements and higher education (in India & Abroad) needs of outgoing students of CMRGI.

The Placement Cell maintains active relationships with top IT, Core Engineering, Product, Services, MBA, and Pharma companies — providing a wide spectrum of opportunities to students across all disciplines.

Pre-placement training including aptitude, communication, group discussions, and mock interviews ensures students are fully equipped for the competitive hiring process.

Skill Development

Pre-Placement Training

Comprehensive training programme designed to make students industry-ready and confident in the campus recruitment process.

Aptitude Training

Quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, verbal ability — aligned with top company patterns including TCS iON, Infosys, Wipro.

Communication Skills

English communication, presentation skills, group discussion, and technical interview preparation sessions with industry experts.

Technical Skill Training

Domain-specific training in Java, Python, Data Structures, DBMS, Web Technologies — workshops by Capgemini, EPAM, Virtusa.

Mock Interviews

HR and technical mock interview sessions with industry professionals, followed by detailed feedback and improvement plans.

Resume Building

Professional resume and LinkedIn profile building workshops — helping students present their best selves to recruiters.

Industry Interaction

Regular industry visits, guest lectures, webinars and internship opportunities with 200+ partner companies.
